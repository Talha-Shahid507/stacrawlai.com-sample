Frequently asked

What counts as a "page"? +

A page is one unique URL crawled and processed. Retries and cached pages don't count against your quota. Screenshots and PDF exports each count as one additional page.

Can I use StackCrawlAI to train AI models? +

Yes. Our output is LLM-ready and suitable for fine-tuning datasets, RAG pipelines, and vector embedding. Check our Terms for specifics on data usage.

Do you support JavaScript-heavy sites? +

Absolutely. We use full Playwright rendering under the hood, so SPAs, infinite scroll, lazy loading, and login-gated pages all work seamlessly.

Is there a free trial of Pro? +

Yes — every new account gets a 14-day Pro trial automatically. No credit card required to start. You'll be downgraded to the free tier if you don't add billing info.
